F A Q s

Got questions? We’ve got answers!

At Sajre Edutech, we understand that learners and participants often have questions

✦ What is the National Art Summit (NAS)?
The National Art Summit (NAS) is a nationwide creative competition organized by Sajre Edutech, designed to give visibility to emerging and professional artists across India. It includes multiple age categories, exciting themes, and attractive rewards.
✦ Who can participate in NAS?
Anyone aged 9 years and above can participate, with categories for Juniors, Teens, Youth, and Professional Artists.
✦ How many rounds are there in NAS?
NAS has three rounds:
  • Round 1 : Online Submission
  • Round 2 : Online Creative Challenge
  • Round 3 : Offline Grand Finale
✦ What are the participation fees?
  • Junior Artists (9–12 years) – ₹399
  • Teen Creators (13–17 years) – ₹499
  • Youth Masters (18–22 years) – ₹799
✦ Can schools, colleges, and organizations enroll their students?
Yes! Institutions can register multiple participants and avail special benefits.
✦ Is there a deadline for registration?
Yes, registrations close before the first round begins. We recommend registering early to confirm your spot.
✦ Is the event online or offline?
Rounds 1 & 2 are online, and Round 3 (Grand Finale) is offline at a designated venue.
✦ Who can I contact for more details?
You can reach us via our official contact page or email us at support@sajreedutech.com.
